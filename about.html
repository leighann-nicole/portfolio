Leighann Darville

Writer / Publisher

Leighann Darville, born in The Bahamas in 1996, moved to the Okanagan in 2023 to pursue a career in writing.

Since they were a child, Leighann was interested in books and the words they contained. Given the climate of The Bahamas, they set that dream aside and settled into accounting as a lifelong career with a maybe about writing on the side. Leighann worked over 6 years at Deloitte & Touche before realising that they needed a change. They packed up their home, spouse, and cats and flew to the Okanagan to attend Okanagan College. Since joining the Writing and Publishing Program they have gained a number of skills which are demonstrated across this website.

Leighann is unemployed and about to graduate.

The future is unknown and full of potential.
